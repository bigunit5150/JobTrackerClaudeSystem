# Mock Interview — Quick Reference

Mock interviews, interviewer-specific prep, and post-interview debriefs all
happen in the **same chat** where you ran the interview prep initialization.
See `prompts/interview-prep-init.md` for the full workflow.

---

## Commands (say these in your interview prep chat)

### Run a Mock Interview

```text
Mock [ROUND TYPE] interview.

Interviewer: [NAME and TITLE if known, otherwise just the round type]
```

Round type options: Phone Screen, Hiring Manager, Panel, Final Round

### Prep for a Specific Interviewer

```text
Prep for [INTERVIEWER NAME], [TITLE] — [ROUND TYPE]
[Optional: LinkedIn URL, anything you know about them]
```

### Debrief After a Real Interview

```text
Debrief [ROUND TYPE] with [INTERVIEWER NAME/TITLE].

Here's how it went:
[Describe the interview — questions asked, how you answered, anything
surprising, your read on how it went]
```

### Update Master Cheat Sheet

```text
Update cheat sheet
[Optional: any new info to incorporate]
```
