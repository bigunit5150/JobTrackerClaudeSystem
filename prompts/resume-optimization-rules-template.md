## 4. Resume Generation Protocol

### Step 1 — Select Base Resume
Analyze the JD for:
- Years of management required
- Team size / org scope
- Reporting level
- Leadership vs. technical emphasis

### Step 2 — Identify Top 5 Hiring Signals
Extract the 5 most important requirements in priority order.

### Step 3 — Map Signals to Experience
For each signal: Strong match / Partial match / Gap
If 2+ signals are gaps: flag as stretch or potential skip.

### Step 4 — Tailor Summary and Core Strengths
Rewrite to address top 2-3 signals using the role's language.

### Step 5 — Reframe Experience Bullets
Prioritize bullets addressing top 5 signals. Mirror JD language naturally.

## 8. Fit Assessment Framework

### 🟢 Green — Proceed and Optimize
- Compensation: meets minimum
- Role level: matches targets
- Location: works
- Signal match: 3+ of top 5 are strong

### 🟡 Yellow — Proceed with Caveats
- Compensation: starts below but reaches minimum
- 1-2 signal gaps addressable in context
- Stretch scope (higher level, larger org)
- New industry but strong transferable skills

### 🔴 Red — Recommend Skip
- Compensation: entirely below minimum
- 3+ major signal gaps
- Requires specialized expertise not in background
- Heavy IC / individual contributor requirement
- Significantly below experience level

**If Red:** [Generate resume if asked anyway / Always decline]
